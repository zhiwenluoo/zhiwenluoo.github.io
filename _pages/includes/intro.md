I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Cybersecurity and Intelligent Systems Engineering (CISE), Concordia University, Montreal, Canada, and have recently passed my Ph.D. defence under the supervision of [Prof. Nizar Bouguila](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=JF6jrN8AAAAJ&hl=en).

My research focuses on **generative models**, **representation learning**, **multimodal learning**, and **clustering**. I am interested in probabilistic and latent-geometry learning for complex data, with recent work on hyperspherical and axial representation learning, deep clustering and neural topic modeling. My work has been published or accepted at venues including KDD, ACM MM, SIGIR, WWW, IJCAI, ICME, ICASSP, *ACM Transactions on Knowledge Discovery from Data*, *Neural Networks*, *Expert Systems with Applications*, *Neurocomputing*, and *Applied Intelligence*.
